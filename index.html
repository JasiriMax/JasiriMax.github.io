02. My Projects

Hospital Management System (HMS)

What it is: A complete software platform for hospitals and clinics to manage their entire operation digitally.

The Goal: To streamline daily tasks for medical staff, reduce paperwork, and improve patient care by making information accessible and secure.

Key Features Explained:

  • HIPAA & GDPR Ready: Built to meet strict international data privacy laws, ensuring all patient information is handled with the highest level of security.
  • Secure Data: Uses advanced encryption (like a digital safe) to protect sensitive medical records.
  • Role-Based Access: Staff can only see the information relevant to their role (e.g., a nurse's view is different from a doctor's), preventing unauthorized access.
